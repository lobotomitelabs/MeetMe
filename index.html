✦ who I am ✦

solo dev with a curiosity cabinet for a brain

I'm Clio — a solo indie developer and digital artist with a thing for dark aesthetics, weird little worlds, projects that blur the line between game and experience and making clothes that are out of this world.

Most of what I build lives somewhere in the gothic sci-fi pocket: pixel art that feels a little too real, UIs that look like they're watching you back, stories that get under your skin, clothes that were made just for you. I like making things that feel like you found them rather than downloaded/bought them.

When I'm not deep in Godot or wrestling with CSS at 4am and 8 monsters deep, I'm probably filling up my curiosity cabinet with references, aesthetics, and games I'll will most definitely finish someday.
